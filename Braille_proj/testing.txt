You're in good company. Last year, we received almost 12,000 applications for Regular Decision and Early Decision II between December 26th and January 1st. That's more than half of our applicants who waited until the last minute to hit submit. This page is dedicated to you: the procrastinators. Below, you'll find everything you need to finish your applications quickly, and finish them well.
